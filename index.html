About Us

PBM Tubes was incorporated in 2019 and has emerged as a leading manufacturer and trader of Stainless Steel Pipes. We are renowned for our extensive range of stainless steel pipes, manufacturing material of 202 & 304 grade, which includes Round Shaped Pipe, Square Shaped Pipe, Rectangle Shaped Pipe, Capsule (Oval) Shaped Pipe, D-Shaped Pipe, and much more. Our products are known for their aesthetic designs, strength, high durability, and quality, as well as their ease of installation and maintenance. We also provide both mirror finish pipes and matt finish pipes, catering to a variety of aesthetic and functional needs. We offer the most efficient and reliable products at very competitive prices, ensuring value for money with outstanding service support. Our ethical business practices and customer-centric approach have won us accolades from our esteemed clientele.
